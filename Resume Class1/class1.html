Emperor Tewodros II was one of the most magnificent Ethiopian leaders and founder of modern Ethiopia.

  • He was born as Kassa Hailu in Quara (Gonder) in 1818.
  • He took power and Emperor of Ethiopia in 1855.
  • Tewodros’ dream of reigning over a united and strong Ethiopia and over Jerusalem.
    • "husband of Ethiopia and fiancée of Jerusalem"
  • The battle of Meqdella took place in April 1867.
    • “I know their game. First, the traders and the missionaries: then the ambassadors: then the cannon. It's better to go straight to the cannon.”
  • The emperor committed suicide on Easter April 13, 1868
    • 'I can’t do that; I can’t see my people pay the price for the wrong that I do.'
